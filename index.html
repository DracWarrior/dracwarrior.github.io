What is this site about?

This is Tech Bias, a satirical look at the persistent gender disparities in technology. Because sometimes humor is the best way to highlight serious issues that continue despite countless "diversity initiatives."

The Brogrammer Culture

Ping pong tables, beer on tap, and "work hard, play hard" cultures that somehow always benefit the same demographic. We explore how tech companies create environments that subtly signal "No Girls Allowed" while claiming to be meritocracies.
