The Mysterious Case of the Disappearing Women in Tech
Where do they all go?

It's definitely not the toxic work environment...

In a shocking discovery that surprised absolutely no one who's been paying attention, recent studies confirm that women continue to leave tech at alarming rates. Industry experts (all men) gathered at a prestigious conference (with all-male panels) to discuss this perplexing phenomenon.

"It's a real mystery," said Chad Broseph, CEO of TechDude Inc., while ignoring the stack of exit interviews citing harassment, unequal pay, and lack of advancement opportunities. "We've tried everything - from mandatory diversity training sessions led by white men to posting empowering quotes on International Women's Day. We even have a ping pong table!"

Meanwhile, the industry continues to puzzle over why women would leave environments where their ideas are attributed to male colleagues, they're mistaken for administrative staff, and they're asked to take notes in meetings despite being senior engineers.
